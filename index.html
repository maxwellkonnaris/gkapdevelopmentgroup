About

GKAP is a full‑service real estate development company with experience and management of multifamily and mixed‑use properties in New York City. GKAP takes an innovative approach to creating and re‑zoning areas of NYC—renovating and repositioning while creating tremendous upside to our projects. Throughout all stages of the development process—from researching locations, due diligence and strategy, to site and building design, construction, branding & marketing—GKAP's seasoned team is well‑versed in every aspect of the development process. GKAP's success is directly tied to its ability to identify unique opportunities, envision a new future for the property, and execute the development at every stage. We are involved in all facets including acquisitions, finance, design, construction, sales & marketing, and project management. Our belief in making great architecture guides our practice.

Partners

Andreas Panagos

Partner

Andreas Panagos is a co‑founder of GKAP. Andreas has been involved in real estate for 20 years, working his way from the ground up. He began his career as a mortgage broker, quickly launching his own mortgage company, then moved into commercial financing and investment sales, developing a keen eye for a property's future value. Andreas directs day‑to‑day operations, overseeing acquisitions, joint ventures, and project management. He is a member of HABA (Hellenic Bankers Association).

George Konnaris

Partner

George Konnaris, co‑founder at GKAP, is a skilled architect with 30 years of experience in the NYC construction industry. His expertise spans architectural and interior design, zoning variance and planning, project management, financing, and coordination. George leads property evaluation, feasibility studies, and building design, coordinating approvals and permits while negotiating construction contracts.

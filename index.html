About

GKAP is a full‑service real‑estate development company with four decades of experience delivering multifamily and mixed‑use properties across New York City. Our Greek‑American founders combine heritage‑driven design principles with modern urban innovation— guiding every stage from site selection and zoning to construction, branding, and leasing.

Partners

Andreas Panagos

Co‑Founder

With 20 years in real‑estate finance and investment sales, Andreas directs day‑to‑day operations—overseeing acquisitions, joint ventures, and project management. He is a member of the Hellenic Bankers Association.

George Konnaris

Co‑Founder & Architect

George brings 30 years of NYC design and construction experience. His work spans zoning variance, feasibility studies, and architectural design, guiding projects from concept to completion.
